# 🚀 LLM Complete Project — Build a Story Generator From Scratch

## One Real-World Project, Every Concept

This is a **single interactive project** that walks you through building a complete AI Story Generator. Every step maps to a real concept from math, science, NLP, and LLM engineering.

**You will NOT need to search anything outside this page.**

## The Project: AI Story Generator

You're building a model that reads stories and writes new ones. Each of the 16 steps teaches a concept by applying it to this real project.

## 4 Phases, 16 Steps

### 🔵 Phase 1: The Math Engine (Steps 1-4)
*The science behind why neural networks work*

| Step | Concept | What You Build | Real-World Connection |
|------|---------|---------------|----------------------|
| 1 | **Vectors & Embeddings** | Word-to-number converter | Every word = a 768-dim vector in GPT |
| 2 | **Matrix Multiplication** | Neural network layer | Every layer = matrix multiply |
| 3 | **Derivatives & Gradients** | Weight updater | Backprop = chain rule through layers |
| 4 | **Probability & Softmax** | Next-word predictor | How LLMs choose the next token |

### 🟣 Phase 2: The Neural Network (Steps 5-8)
*Building the brain of the story generator*

| Step | Concept | What You Build | Real-World Connection |
|------|---------|---------------|----------------------|
| 5 | **Neurons & Activations** | Processing layer (ReLU, GELU, SwiGLU) | Every transformer block has these |
| 6 | **Attention Mechanism** | Context-aware layer (Q, K, V) | THE innovation behind all LLMs |
| 7 | **Transformer Block** | Complete processing unit | GPT = stack of these blocks |
| 8 | **Full Architecture** | Complete model (embed → blocks → output) | This IS a mini-GPT |

### 🟠 Phase 3: Training & Optimization (Steps 9-12)
*Making the model actually learn*

| Step | Concept | What You Build | Real-World Connection |
|------|---------|---------------|----------------------|
| 9 | **Tokenization (Char + BPE)** | Text splitter | GPT uses BPE with 50K vocab |
| 10 | **Training Loop** | Learning engine (AdamW, LR schedule) | The 5-line loop that trains any model |
| 11 | **Regularization** | Overfitting prevention (dropout, weight decay, clipping) | Essential for generalization |
| 12 | **Evaluation** | Quality measurement (perplexity, loss curves) | How to know if your model is good |

### 🔴 Phase 4: Advanced & Production (Steps 13-16)
*Cutting-edge techniques used by GPT-4, LLaMA, Mistral*

| Step | Concept | What You Build | Real-World Connection |
|------|---------|---------------|----------------------|
| 13 | **Advanced Attention** | RoPE, GQA, KV-Cache, Flash Attention | LLaMA, Mistral use all of these |
| 14 | **Generation Strategies** | Sampler (top-k, top-p, beam search, speculative) | How ChatGPT generates text |
| 15 | **Scaling & Efficiency** | Optimizer (MoE, LoRA, quantization, scaling laws) | How to train/serve billion-param models |
| 16 | **Alignment & Safety** | Preference tuner (RLHF, DPO) | How ChatGPT becomes helpful and safe |
